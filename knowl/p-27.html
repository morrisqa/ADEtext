In this section (and the next few sections), we will concern ourselves with initial value problems of the form

\begin{equation} \left\{ \begin{array}{l} x^\prime = f(t,x) \\ x(\alpha)=\beta \end{array} \right.\tag{1.2.1} \end{equation}

where \(\alpha,\beta \in \mathbb{R}\) and \(f:D \to \mathbb{R}^n\) is a continuous function defined on an open set \(D=I \times \mathbb{R} \subset \mathbb{R}^2\) with \(\alpha \in I\text{.}\)
